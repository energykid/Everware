[h1]Everware[/h1]

-+=+-

[h2]this mod adds content, no restrictions.[/h2]

no set theme - any addition or change is fair game so long as i want to create it!

i try my best to make sure attention is given to details and take care to make content feel at home in Terraria while giving it my own spin.

current content in Everware spans a short portion of the game.

this includes two interchangeable world structures of which you get one per world: the Kiln and the Quarry.
i have also reworked the Eye of Cthulhu, and added extra loot to the Underworld.

extra care has been taken to hone as many details as possible in the content!

[b]once downloaded, check the mod's in-game description for more details![/b]
