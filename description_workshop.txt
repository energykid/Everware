[h1]Everware[/h1]

-+=+-

Everware is a work-in-progress mod which, at large, adds general content, improvements to vanilla, and reworks for some content!

i try my best to make sure attention is given to details and take care to make content feel at home in Terraria, while still giving it my own spin.

currently, this mod adds two interchangeable world structures of which you get one per world: the Kiln and the Quarry.
i have also reworked the Eye of Cthulhu, and added extra loot to the Underworld.

extra care has been taken to hone as many details as possible!

[b]once downloaded, check the mod's in-game description for more details![/b]
