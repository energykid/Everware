Everware v0.1 - humble beginnings

added Kilnstone armor and the Kilnpost, craftable only at the Kiln

Everware v0.1.4 - polishing things up before 0.2

added a new menu, "Somewhere Else," with a custom OST and logo
added music boxes for all existing content with associated music
you can craft the music box for the new menu theme using all other music boxes in the mod
various small balancing and gameplay changes

Everware v0.2.0 - bloody hell update

reworked the Eye of Cthulhu, adding new music as well
